DJP Ke 5 Mudde (Manifesto)

Jo hum kehte hain, woh hum poora karte hain!

1

Total Security (Wafadari aur Suraksha)

Jaise ek kutta apne ghar ke liye jaan de deta hai, waise hi sarkar har nagrik ki suraksha ki guarantee legi.

2

Kamgar & Small Business Support

Cyber cafes aur chhote business ke liye low taxes aur majdooron ke liye fixed health insurance ka kanoon.

3

Purna Dharm-Nirpekshata (Secularism)

Dharam ke naam par rajneeti ka khatma, sabhi dharmo ke parva aur sanskriti ko barabar badhava.

4

Yuva Rozgar & Digital Skills

Har yuva ke liye free advanced AI aur digital skill courses aur business setup ke liye zero-interest loans.

5

Free Health & Hunger-Free Society

Zarooratmand parivaaron ko free quality food aur har gaon mein muft medical clinic ki vyavastha.
